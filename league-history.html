Current Ruleset
League Structure
  • 10 teams, ESPN platform
  • Communication via Discord
  • 14-week regular season
  • 3-week playoffs, 6 teams qualify
  • Top 2 seeds receive a first-round bye
Buy-In & Payouts
  • $50 per owner per season
  • 1st place: $210
  • 2nd place: $100
  • 3rd place: $50
  • Weekly high score: $10
  • Consolation dollar: $1 to 2nd-highest scorer if they lost
Scoring
  • Half-PPR scoring (0.5 pts per reception)
  • Fractional / decimal scoring enabled
  • Missed PATs: −0.5 pts
  • Forced fumble: 1 pt
  • Recovered fumble: 2 pts
Keepers
  • 1 keeper per team
  • Drafted players kept at their draft round
  • Waiver pickups kept at ADP + 1 round
  • Max 3 seasons rostered
  • Cannot keep 1st-round picks
  • Duplicate round values: forfeit the round above
  • Player contracts reset after trades
Draft
  • Snake draft, 60-second pick clock
  • Lottery: every team gets 2 balls
  • 10th: 48 balls · 9th: 22 · 8th: 11 · 7th: 7
  • Draft pick trading enabled
Rules & Admin
  • Trade deadline: beginning of Week 13
  • ESPN "undroppable" restrictions disabled
  • 2 IR slots
  • IR violation ladder: 1st = 12th round pick, 2nd = 8th, 3rd = 5th
  • Inactive manager: commissioner may set lineup after 2 weeks

2025
  • Draft Lottery modified: every team gets 2 balls. 10th place gets 48, 9th gets 22, 8th gets 11, 7th gets 7. Remaining teams get 2 each.
  • IR IR violation penalty ladder introduced: 1st offense = loss of 12th round pick, 2nd = 8th round, 3rd = 5th round.
  • Schedule Rivalry week removed from schedule.
2024
  • Rules No league changes in 2024.
2023
  • Teams League expanded from 8 → 10 teams.
    👋 Joe and John join the league.
  • Payouts Playoffs increased: $210 / $100 / $50. Weekly high score increased $5 → $10. Consolation dollar unchanged at $1.
  • Playoffs Switched to 3-week format with 6 teams; top 2 seeds receive a first-round bye.
  • Schedule Rivalry week added to regular season schedule.
  • Trades Trade deadline pushed to the beginning of Week 13.
2022
  • Rules No league changes in 2022.
2021
  • Buy-In Increased $40 → $50 per owner.
  • Payouts Playoffs increased: $185 / $90 / $50.
  • Schedule Regular season extended by 1 week after the NFL added Week 18. Final week of playoffs now week 17.
  • Scoring Forced fumble = 1 pt · Recovered fumble = 2 pts.
  • Keepers Waiver keeper rules unified: all waiver pickups (drafted or undrafted) kept for ADP+1.
  • IR Covid IR slot converted to a general-purpose 2nd IR slot.
  • Draft Draft order adjusted so mid-round picks are treated as worst.
2020
  • Buy-In Increased to $41.25 to account for 2 additional weeks of weekly high score payouts.
  • Payouts Consolation dollar introduced: $1 to the 2nd-highest scorer each week — but only if they lost.
  • Playoffs Switched from 2-week to 1-week playoff format.
  • Scoring Forced fumble = 1.5 pts · Recovered fumble = 1.5 pts.
  • Waivers ESPN's "undroppable player" restrictions disabled.
  • Keepers Player contracts reset after trades.
  • IR Covid IR slot added.
2019
  • Platform League communication switched from Facebook → Discord.
  • Keepers Keeper count reduced from 2 → 1. Waiver pickups kept for ADP+1 (previously Round 15).
  • Scoring Missed PATs = −0.5 pts.
  • Trades Trade deadline set to just after Week 10.
  • Draft Pick time increased back to 60 seconds.
2018
  • Teams 8 teams
    Brian is forced into retirement and is replaced by Johnny A.
  • Scoring Switched to half-PPR scoring.
  • Keepers Complex keeper rules: drafted players kept by round, dropped players kept in Rounds 5 / 10 / 15 based on original draft round.
  • Draft Draft pick trading enabled. Pick time reduced to 45 seconds.
  • IR 1 IR slot added.
  • Trades Trade deadline removed.
2017
  • Teams 8 teams
    Brandon retires and is replaced by Ben.
  • Keepers Waiver pickups can be kept for 15th round pick.
2016
  • Major Formal rulebook introduced — the league's first written constitution. It is highly respected.
  • Teams League contracted from 10 → 8 teams.
    Loren and Rob retire.
  • Buy-In Increased to $40 per owner.
  • Payouts Playoffs: $150 / $70 / $40 · $5/week high score
  • Draft Lottery introduced: 8th gets 55 balls, 7th gets 25, 6th gets 12, 5th gets 8. Others get 2 each.
  • Keepers Keepers introduced: 2 per team, max 3 seasons rostered. Cannot keep 1st-round picks. Duplicate round values forfeit the round above.
  • Trades Trade deadline formally introduced.
2015
  • Teams League drops from 12 to 10 teams.
    Danielle, Keegan, and Bob retire. Bob takes an advisory role on Carl's team. 👋 Rob joins.
  • Scoring Fractional / decimal scoring implemented.
  • Buy-In Increased to $26 per owner.
  • Payouts Playoffs: $109 / $60 / $31. Weekly high score prize introduced: $5 paid to the highest scorer each week (12-week season).
  • Inactive League manager may set lineup for inactive members after 2 weeks (from current roster only).
  • Draft Pick time set to 60 seconds.
2014
  • Name League renamed to "Monday Morning Tears".
  • Teams 12 teams.
    Mike H & Jim R retire. 👋 Ray, Brian, Brandon, and Bob join the league.
  • Buy-In $5 per owner (first year with a buy-in).
  • Payouts Playoffs: $35 / $15 / $10 (unconfirmed).
2013 — League Founded
  • Founded League created as "Madame DeRuiter's Pauvre Bebes"
  • Teams 10 teams.
    Bill, Mike, Andy, Carl, Phil, Loren, Danielle, Keegan, Jim R, Mike H
  • Platform Facebook group used for league communication and trash talk.
  • Buy-In No buy-in/payout — bragging rights only.
  • Scoring Standard scoring with bonuses.
  • Schedule Season started in Week 2 of the NFL calendar.
